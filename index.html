ABOUT US
& OUR HISTORY

//UNDER CONSTRUCTION!//
The German eSports organisation BIERAIMING was founded on the 25th of November in 2014 by burnz0 and kenshinHAN. Headquartered in Leipzig, the BIERAIMING organistion spans across whole Germany and Austria.

The idea behind BIERAIMING goes way back to the golden times of Counterstrike 1.6 when both founders had noticed that drinking BIER improved their aim to a certain degree. So they came up with the word composition BIERAIMING to describe this incident. What they did not know back then - it is an scientifically proven effect which is shown in the following graph:


Since most of the members are German, it was obvious to choose the native expression 'BIER' instead of the English word ‘beer’.
We are having two teams right now who are taking part in the 99Dmg league. The pro team is currently playing in Division 3.3 and the amateur team has participated in Starter Division #102 and will be playing in Division 5 next season.

We are more than just a team - we are an open-minded community of people who are having fun together playing counterstrike and - of course - we really like drinking BIER.
